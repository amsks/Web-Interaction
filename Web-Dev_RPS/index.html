MANIPAL

Location

Manipal is a scenic town located in the Udupi City, in the South-Indian State of Karnataka. It neighbors the dsitricts of Kundapura on one side which acts as an entry point to the Western Ghats, and also is not very far from the districts of Malpe and Pithrody, which are the coastal areas on the coast of Arabian Sea

Climate

Due to its proximity to the Rainforests of the Western Ghats and to the costal areas of the Arabian sea, Manipal experiences a heavy dose of rains throughout the year, which is usually for about a period of 6-8 months. The other months are full of humid heat that is common to these areas of South India.

About Me

My association with Manipal has been for about four years. It is the place where I pursued my Bachelor's degree in the field of Electronics and Communication Engineering. My father is in a job that requires a high degree of mobility, and so I never spend more than 2-3 years in one single place during my school life. Thus, even though I was born in the capital of India, New Delhi, I never spent much time there. Thus, Manipal closest to a Hometown to me, both in terms of the time spent and the impact it has had on me. This impact was not just limited to studies, but also to the places I used to visit, the friends I made during those four years, the people I met and learned hundreds of things from, etc. It has imbibed in me a desire to travel different places around the world and expand my experience of those places by not just seeing the things that everyone tells you to see, but also see it through the lens of someone living there, by making new friends. Manipal has been central in my ability to -connect with people, learn new things, and become a better leader.
